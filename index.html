About Me

I'm Raffa Shalsabila, an Information Systems student at UIN Jakarta with a keen interest in design, especially graphic design and user interface (UI). I am interested in how design can improve user experience as well as make technology more accessible and engaging. My talent lies in the ability to combine aesthetics with functionality in design.

I am proficient in various design tools such as Canva and have attended various workshops and trainings related to graphic design and UX/UI to hone my skills. I am committed to continuously developing my design skills by learning the latest trends in design and technology. I hope to contribute to the world of digital design by creating innovative and user-friendly solutions.

Although my main focus is UI/UX, I also have a great curiosity towards other fields in technology, such as web development, data analysis, and artificial intelligence/machine learning. I believe that understanding different aspects of technology can enrich my perspective as a designer and help me create more well-rounded and impactful digital solutions.

Projects

BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE DIBIMBING BOOTCAMP

UI/UX GDGOC UIN Jakarta

In this project, my team and I created a modern platform for Islamic learning to access accurate and easy-to-understand Islamic materials. My role as a UX Designer is to ensure that the user experience is easy, comfortable and enjoyable when using an app or website.

WEB DEVELOPER HITLAB

My team and I developed a web-based climate change education platform that provides up-to-date information, statistical data, and interactive tools such as a carbon footprint calculator to help people understand environmental issues and encourage action to reduce them.
